## About this Archive

**Remembering Old Roblox** is a digital archive documenting discontinued and transformed features of early Roblox.

It focuses on platform systems such as economy, interface design, communication tools, and digital culture.

---

## Purpose

This project explores how online platforms change over time and how digital experiences are preserved, altered, or removed.
